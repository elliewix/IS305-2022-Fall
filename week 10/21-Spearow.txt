Eats bugs in grassy areas. It has to flap its short wings at high speed to stay airborne. Spearows are said to have short temperaments, and tend to swarm perceived enemies in large flocks. This is an alternative and sometimes considered a rival to Pidgey. Ash Ketchum attempted to catch a Spearow without the help of his Pikachu, leading to a rather disastrous start of his journey.
