Yamper are corgi-like Pokémon often used for herding, and assists people with the intention of receiving treats as a reward. with the unique ability "Ball Fetch", which retrieves used Poké Balls. Upon its initial reveal, Yamper received positive reactions, with critics and fans calling it adorable.[14][15][16] Some have noted that Yamper's design may reference Queen Elizabeth II's fondness for Welsh and Pembroke corgis.[17]
