A Mythical Pokémon introduced in Pokémon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon initially discovered through data mining and officially revealed on 8 April 2018 through a trailer for Pokémon the Movie: The Power of Us.[42] It is available only through special event distributions. It has a signature move, called Plasma Fists, that changes Normal-type moves into Electric ones on contact. After Plasma Fits is used, it causes a deluge of ions to rain down on the battlefield.
