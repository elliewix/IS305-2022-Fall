Eevee is often considered among the "cutest" Pokémon in the franchise.[71][72][73][74] Because of this, Eevee is featured on various Pokémon-related merchandise.[75][76][77] Mascot of Pokémon: Let's Go, Eevee! Eevee has an exclusive evolution style, known a Eeveelutions, where each evolution requires special requirements to occur, and is available in almost all types, except Ghost, Dragon, Steel, and Flying.
