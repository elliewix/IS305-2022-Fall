Poliwhirl is among the most-marketed Pokémon,[26] being used in a line of chewable vitamins as well as a line of toothbrushes. Poliwhirl appears as a humanoid shaped Pokémon with a similar appearance to Poliwag, except it has arms and no tail. It evolves into Poliwrath only after being exposed to a Water Stone. It also evolves into Politoed after being traded while holding a King's Rock.[27][28]
