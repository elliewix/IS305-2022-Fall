A pink Pokémon adorned with ribbons and butterfly-like bows,[52] Sylveon use their ribbon-like feelers to soothe trainers and Pokémon alike.[53] It is the eighth branching evolution to Generation I's Eevee. It was revealed on February 14, 2013, before the official announcement of Fairy-type.[52] Sylveon was designed by Atsuko Nishida.[54]
