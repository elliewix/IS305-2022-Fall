"Land Forme"Shaymin has a second form activated by using a Gracidea flower in the daytime. Shaymin's Sky Forme will revert into its Land Forme during the night, while frozen solid, or if it is deposited in the PC. Its Land Forme is based on a hedgehog. To avoid detction, it curls up to look like a pach of grass. It absorbs toxin to dissolve them into the air.
