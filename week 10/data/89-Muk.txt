Muk's name comes from the corruption of the word, "muck". A sludge Pokémon that is also be based on Dorotabō, a one-eyed, three-fingered yōkai that rises from the mud of neglected, overgrown rice fields. Its body smells so awful, it can cause fainting. Through degeneration, it lost its sense of smell. Muk has an Alola Form that is an Poison/Dark Type. Like Grimer, Alolan Muk has multiple colors, and now razor sharp crystal like teeth.
